Internal Operations System

Branch Manager (BM)

  • Responsible for monitoring Operation, Sales & Financial matters.
  • Can directly check with operation staff on updates.
  • Responsible for daily reporting to the General Manager.
  • Responsible for branch administration, accounting, operations, and sales.

Marketing Coordinator (MC)

  • Monitor all shipments from booking to return of empty containers.
  • Update operations, clients & other parties to the last details of cargo status.
  • Require Operation Staff to give reports and updates (Strict Compliance).
  • Issue HOUSE WAY BILL for every single transaction.

Operation Staff (Terminal)

  • Execute required reports, updates, and status of shipments.
  • Container status report should be forwarded to the Marketing Coordinator.
  • Report to the MC/CSR status on trucking, vessel, pertaining documents.
  • Liaison officer is responsible for updating reports from Banks, Billings, etc.
